Investigation Overview¶

The main goal here is trying to find the users who use the bike sharing and when.

Dataset Overview¶

This data set is taken from https://www.fordgobike.com/system-data and represents trips taken by members of the bike sharing service by FORD for month of February of 2019. Data consists of info about trips taken by service's members, their types, their birth year, their gender, stations of starting and ending trips, duration of trips etc.

We here find that 90.2% of trips don't share the bike and only 9.8% sharing bikes
